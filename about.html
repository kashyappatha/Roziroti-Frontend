DEI Statement

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

At 24 Carrots, we are people-powered. The collective sum of the individual differences, knowledge, life experiences, innovation, talents, diverse backgrounds, and unique capabilities our team members provide to 24 Carrots is what makes us unique, drives our culture, built our reputation, and is the root of all our accomplishments. We strive for a diverse, inclusive, and equitable workplace where all employees, whatever their gender, gender identity, race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, age, sexual orientation, education, disability, veteran status, or other characteristics, feel valued, respected, cared for, and have the proper platform for both personal and professional growth.

Our community is what propels us to be better in everything we do. Therefore, our core values and beliefs are not confined to just our team. As an industry and community leader, we have the responsibility and the passion to nurture a spirit of inclusion and drive cultural change for the better. As the world grows, so shall we. We have made the commitment to be always evolving and ever-learning, ensure our team has a voice, and support the voice of others.

A Name Synonymous with Integrity

Over the years, 24 Carrots has built a stellar reputation based on our honest approach to business and unwavering commitment to excellence. We refuse to compromise on quality and our friendly, team-driven culture complements our award-winning food. We’re proud to have earned both the respect of our peers and the continued business of our loyal customers.

Refreshing Ideas

The 24 Carrots team of experts aren’t interested in just meeting expectations. We want to make jaws drop! Our creative staff and talented partners continually push for imaginative new ideas, making us one of the top trendsetters in the catering and events industry.

The Right Fit

We’re not a big conglomerate that churns out cookie-cutter parties. And we’re not a small mom-and-pop shop that lacks the resources to ensure your event runs smoothly. 24 Carrots is right in the middle – the sweet spot that provides you with the utmost personal attention and unlimited possibilities for your one-of-a-kind occasion.

Genuine Relationships

Even as we’ve grown into an award-winning industry leader, 24 Carrots has stayed true to our values as a family-owned business. For us, it’s all about personal connections and everything we do centers around our passion for bringing people together and creating unforgettable memories.

giving back

24 Cares

We wholeheartedly believe that a generous spirit unites our community. Our 24 Carrots Culture Committee leads community service events throughout the year, dedicating our time to various causes and philanthropic work.

24 Carrots is proud to partner with Second Harvest Food Bank of Orange County, a local non-profit organization that provides redistributed meals to the hungry. By donating our leftover and unused food to this great local program, every one of our events makes a difference in our community!
